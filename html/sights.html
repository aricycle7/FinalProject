See the wonders of Columbus Ohio

Tour the German Village and head over to
The Book Loft, a slice of history with 32 rooms
to get lost in.
Spend the day at the Columbus Zoo and Aquarium, see the animals in all their glory
See the Franklin Park Conservatory and marvel at the wonderful gardens.

Looking for somthing a bit more strange? Look no further than the Field Of Corn. 109 concrete ears of corn in a feild.

If you're a fan of art you should check out the Columbus Museum of Art and admire the beautiful exhibits displayed.

If you're a fan of sports be sure to check out the historic Horseshoe Statium, home of THE Ohio State Buckeyes.
